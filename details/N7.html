Description

This is a single shrine of the octagonal plan in this temple complex. 4 tiers of roof are at least remaining. Stucco and pigment partially remain on the Flying Palace. Excavation survey in the main chamber in 2003. Pillar holes were confirmed on the brick floor at corners of the pedestal. In other words, we can conjecture that the wooden canopy was installed for covering the deity on the pedestal. Looted hole was confirmed under the lowest layer of the large round pedestal, but no object was found through the excavation survey of this hole. Front step was restored in 2007. One lintel in Sambor Prei Kuk style was unearthed from the front of this shrine. Broken pedestal fragments that were collected by the excavation in the chamber were reconstructed with a small amount of new sandstone in 2007-2008. Temporary support was added at the inner opening in 2012. (All works were done by SCP)
